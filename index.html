Idaho

Idaho is a scenic state in the northwestern United States known for its breathtaking mountains, clear rivers, dense forests, and abundant outdoor recreation. Famous for its world-class potatoes, Idaho also offers hiking, fishing, skiing, camping, and wildlife viewing, making it a popular destination for both adventure seekers and those looking to enjoy its peaceful natural beauty.
